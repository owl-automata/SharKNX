SharKNX

Mobile KNX monitoring & diagnostics

SharKNX is a mobile tool for KNX installers, system integrators, and technical maintenance teams. Perform fast diagnostics and live bus monitoring directly on site — without opening ETS or a laptop.

  • Live monitoring of KNX group telegrams and bus traffic
  • View ETS (.knxproj) project data on your phone or tablet
  • Discover and connect to KNX/IP gateways and routers
  • Quick on-site troubleshooting during commissioning and support
