# Creating a Hunt

Tapping the **+** FAB on the [Shark Hunts Page](shark-hunt.md#shark-hunts-page) opens the hunt creation wizard. The wizard guides you through 5 steps to configure your hunt. You can navigate freely between steps at any time, save your progress and continue later, or cancel to discard changes.

> [!TIP]
> You can save a partially completed hunt and continue editing it later from the [Shark Hunts Page](shark-hunt.md#shark-hunts-page).

This guide covers each step of the wizard in detail. For a full reference of all available action types, see the [Actions Reference](actions.md) guide.
