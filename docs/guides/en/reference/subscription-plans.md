# Subscription Plans

SharKNX is a paid app. All features are included in every paid plan — there are no feature tiers.

<img src="../../../../assets/screenshots/guides/reference/subscription-plans/subscription-plans-paywall.png" width="320" alt="SharKNX plan picker" />

---

## Plans

| Plan | Billing | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **SharKNX Monthly** | Recurring monthly | 14-day free trial on first subscription |
| **SharKNX Yearly** | Recurring yearly | 14-day free trial · saves ~16% vs monthly |
| **SharKNX Ultimate** | One-time payment | Lifetime access, never expires |

Pricing is set by the platform store (App Store, Google Play) and varies by region. Current prices are shown in the plan picker inside the app.

---

## What's included

All plans unlock the full feature set:

- ETS Project Viewer (.knxproj import)
- KNX Bus Monitor
- Device Diagnostics
- Program Individual Addresses
- Line Scan
- Reconstruct Device Communication
- Control KNX Installations
- Shark Hunts
- Export (CSV, PDF, TXT, JSON)

## Free trial

### 2-day app preview

Every new install comes with a **2-day free preview**. No subscription or payment is required. The preview starts the first time the app connects to the internet after install and cannot be extended by reinstalling — the timer is tied to the device via a stable identifier.

When the preview expires, a paywall is shown and the app requires an active subscription to continue.

### 14-day subscription trial

The **Monthly** and **Yearly** plans each include a **14-day free trial**. Billing only begins after the trial ends. You can cancel before the trial ends without being charged. The 14-day trial is only available the first time you subscribe; it does not apply to renewals or plan switches.

## Managing your subscription

Subscription management is handled through the platform store. In-app options are available in **Settings → Subscription**.

<img src="../../../../assets/screenshots/guides/reference/subscription-plans/subscription-plans-settings.png" width="320" alt="Subscription section in Settings" />

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Purchase** | Use the plan picker in the app (paywall or Settings → Subscription) |
| **Restore purchases** | Settings → Subscription → **Restore Purchases** — use this after reinstalling or switching devices |
| **Cancel** | Settings → Subscription → **Cancel Subscription** — opens the App Store or Google Play subscription management page |
| **Upgrade to Yearly** | Settings → Subscription → **Upgrade Plan** — shown when on a Monthly plan |
| **Upgrade to Lifetime** | Settings → Subscription → **Upgrade Plan** — shown when on a Yearly plan |

Cancelling stops renewal at the end of the current billing period. Access remains active until the period ends.

---

## Grandfathered access

Users who downloaded SharKNX before the subscription model was introduced retain **lifetime access** at no charge. The Settings → Subscription page shows this as **SharKNX Ultimate (Legacy)**. No action is required.

---

## Offline behaviour

If the app cannot reach the subscription server and no valid cached state exists, it shows an **Unable to Verify** status. The app does not lock you out in this state — connect to the internet and tap **Restore Purchases** to re-verify your subscription.
